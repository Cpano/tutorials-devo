How to register your Free Tier account

To register your free OCI account ($500 USD without needing a credit card):

1. Visit the signup page and enter your account information with the email you registered with when signing up at 314piday.com. Click the Verify my email.

2. Click Select Offer when presented with the offer for $500 in free credits.

3. Visit your email account and look for the Oracle Cloud email asking you to verify your email address and create your Oracle Cloud account.

4. Enter the required information, along with your cloud account name. After you click Start my free trial, your account will be fully provisioned and ready for the labs!
